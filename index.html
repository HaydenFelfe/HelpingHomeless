Welcome to our website! We are dedicated to assisting individuals in California who are in need of homelessness resources. Our website provides a curated list of local homeless shelters within a 5,000-meter radius of your current location. You can easily find shelter names, addresses, and even view their locations on Google Maps.

We are continuously expanding our services to include more resources in the future, such as food bank locations and job training programs. Our goal is to provide comprehensive support to address various needs. If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out to us via email.

Thank you for visiting our website! We hope that our resources can make a positive impact and assist you on your journey towards finding stability and a brighter future.
